Unique Style of Gaming PCs

While similar in specs, each PC case within the MR Series features a strikingly different aesthetic design. The Element MR is designed with a familiar flat face, while the Slate and Trace MR both have three-dimensional crystalline shapes. The Slate MR is constructed with three separate asymmetrical tempered glass panels, while the Trace MR is composed of clean edges that come together to form a symmetrical trapezoid.

ABOUT THE MIRROR SERIES GAMING PCS

Reflect your unique style while showcasing your gaming tower's impressive internals with the Element, Slate, and Trace MR Series of gaming pc cases. Designed for pc gamers with an eye for aesthetics, the MR Series features gorgeous RGB internals, tempered glass side panels, and a unique "partial mirror" finish.
